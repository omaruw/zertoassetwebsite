How the Curriculum Works

One deliberate path, multiple destinations

Every professional starts at C1. It's the decision framework — you'll deep-dive into four business models, score each one against your constraints, and make one clear choice. From there, the curriculum branches into two paths — Physical Product Business (Amazon FBA, with DTC coming later) or Knowledge & Digital Products — depending on where your Fit Test results lead you.

1

Start Here: C1

Complete the foundational course. Choose your model. Build your 30-Day roadmap. Know your decision signals. This is mandatory for everyone.

2

Choose Your Path

After C1, your Fit Test results point you to either Path 1 (Physical Product — starting with Amazon FBA) or Path 2 (Knowledge & Digital Products). No guessing. Data-driven.

3

Execute & Scale

Operate your asset. Apply the frameworks. Document your results. Real assets take time. Real execution beats theory every time.

4

Prove & Decide

Once your parallel income is stable and growing, you've earned the right to make the big decision. C4 teaches the exit framework.

Why This Structure?

Most people fail at building parallel income because they choose the wrong model first. ZeroToAsset inverts this: You learn to assess yourself, then pick the right path. C1 is the gate. It prevents wasted time and wrong choices downstream.

The unlock model creates natural progression and ensures you've actually completed the assessment before moving into advanced execution and exit planning. This is how you build professional-grade results.
