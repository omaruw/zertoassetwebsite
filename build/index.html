Frequently Asked Questions

Honest answers to the questions you should be asking

Do I need to quit my job to start?

No. This platform is built specifically for employed professionals. The courses are designed around 2-3 hours per week. Your career is an asset, not an obstacle.

What if I decide not to start a side business?

That's a valid outcome. Course 1 is a decision framework — it helps you determine whether to proceed, which model fits, or whether now is not the right time. Clarity is the product.

How is this different from other online business courses?

Most courses sell you one model and assume it fits everyone. We start with your constraints — time, capital, risk tolerance, family obligations — and eliminate models that don't fit before you invest anything.

Do I need any prior business experience?

No. The framework assumes you're smart and analytical (you're a professional) but new to evaluating online business models. Everything is explained from first principles.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes. 30-day no-questions-asked refund. If the framework doesn't help you make a clearer decision, you shouldn't pay for it.

Why are the courses self-paced instead of live?

Because you're a professional with a schedule. Cohort-based programs create manufactured urgency. Your career doesn't pause for a course. Learn when it fits your life.
