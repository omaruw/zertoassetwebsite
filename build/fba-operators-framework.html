87% of Amazon FBA Businesses Fail. Here's Why.

They start with $5,000

Real minimum is $15,000. Undercapitalization kills more businesses than bad products. You need runway to weather PPC scaling, inventory holds, and market validation. Starting lean isn't brave—it's risky.

They think it's passive

Launch requires 15–25 hours/week. Daily monitoring is non-negotiable even at maturity. You're not building a passive asset; you're building a business. Some operators eventually step back, but not until it's systematized.

They chase tactics, not systems

Tactics expire. PPC strategies change, market dynamics shift, algorithms update. Decision systems, risk frameworks, and operational discipline endure. This course builds the latter, not the former.

This Course Builds Operators, Not Sellers
